  3. 1/2 sdm paprika bubuk
  4. 1/2 sdt Italian Herbs
  5. 1/4 sdt Parsley
  6. 1 Sdm olive oil. Secukupnya Salt n Pepper

  8. Secukupnya Ghee
  9. 1/4 Bombay
  10. 1 Bawang Putih
  11. Italian herbs n Parsley
  12. Lemon
