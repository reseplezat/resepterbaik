Chicken Burrito

Dipos pada March 2, 2022

Anda sedang mencari inspirasi resep Chicken Burrito yang unik? Cara membuatnya memang susah-susah gampang. Jika keliru mengolah maka hasilnya tidak akan memuaskan dan justru cenderung tidak enak. Padahal Chicken Burrito yang enak harusnya sih memiliki aroma dan rasa yang mampu memancing selera kita.

Banyak hal yang sedikit banyak berpengaruh terhadap kualitas rasa dari Chicken Burrito, mulai dari jenis bahan, selanjutnya pemilihan bahan segar, sampai cara membuat dan menyajikannya. Tidak usah pusing kalau ingin menyiapkan Chicken Burrito enak di rumah, karena asal sudah tahu triknya maka hidangan ini dapat jadi suguhan spesial.

Nah, kali ini kita coba, yuk, variasikan Chicken Burrito sendiri di rumah. Tetap dengan bahan yang sederhana, sajian ini bisa memberi manfaat dalam membantu menjaga kesehatan tubuh kita. Anda bisa membuat Chicken Burrito memakai 31 jenis bahan dan 6 langkah pembuatan. Berikut ini cara dalam membuat hidangannya.

Source: RecipeTinEats

Bahan-bahan dan bumbu yang perlu dipersiapkan untuk membuat Chicken Burrito:

  1. 6 Tortillas
  2. 2 cup White rice
  3. 280 gr Frozen corn, thawed
  4. 150 gr Cheddar cheese
  5. 1/4 cup Cilantro
  6. Burrito Seasoning:
  7. 1 tsp Onion powder
  8. 1 tsp Dried oregano
  9. 2 tsp Cumin powder
  10. 2 tsp Paprika powder
  11. 1/2 tsp Cayenne pepper
  12. 3/4 tsp Salt
  13. 1/4 tsp Black pepper
  14. 1 tbsp Olive oil
  15. Chicken Filling:
  16. 600 gr Chicken breast, sliced in half horizontally
  17. 1/2 Sweet Onion, diced
  18. 1 Red bell pepper, diced
  19. 2 cloves Garlic, finely minced
  20. 1 can Refried beans
  21. 2 tbsp Olive oil
  22. 1/4 cup water
  23. Avocado Sauce:
  24. 1 Large ripe avocado
  25. 1/3 cup Sour cream/plain yoghurt
  26. 1/2 clove Garlic
  27. Juice of 1/2 Lemon
  28. 1 tbsp Olive oil
  29. 1/2 cup Cilantro, roughly chopped
  30. to taste Salt
  31. to taste Black pepper

Langkah-langkah untuk membuat Chicken Burrito

1
Burrito Seasoning: Mix all ingredients, then drizzle olive oil over chicken, mix to coat. Sprinkle with seasoning, toss to coat.
2
Chicken Filling: Heat 1 tbsp oil in a large skillet over high heat. Add chicken, cook 2 minutes. Turn and cook 1.5 minutes. Remove, rest 2 minutes then chop into bite size. In the same skillet, add onion and garlic, cook 1 minute. Add red bell pepper, cook 2 minutes until onion is translucent. Add refried beans, diced chicken and water. Stir and cook for 2 minutes until reduces slightly. Should be thick and juicy, not watery and not dry/stodgy! Let cool 5 minutes - will thicken.
3
Avocado Sauce: Combine all ingredients in a food processor, blend until very smooth. Adjust lemon as required, should have a bit of tang if intended to be paired with rich, bold flavoured food like Mexican food. Keeps for 4 days in an airtight container - and stays green!
4
Assembly Burritos: Gently warm tortillas in microwave (makes them pliable), lay on work surface. Place rice on lower third, top with Chicken Filling, corn, cheese then sprinkle with coriander. Fold up, burrito style.
5
Toasting Burritos: Either pan fry over medium high heat (no oil) for 2 minutes each side to make tortilla crispy and melt cheese a bit OR wrap tightly in foil and bake in oven 10 minutes at 180°C/350°F.
6
Freezing Burritos: Wrap in cling wrap or foil, fridge for 4 days or freeze. Best to warm in oven - 15 minutes at 350°F wrapped in foil. Frozen - thaw overnight then warm in oven 15 minutes. To reheat from frozen, wrap in foil and place in oven for 40 minutes at 350°F.
